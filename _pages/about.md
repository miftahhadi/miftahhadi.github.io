My full name is Miftah Hadi Syahputra Anfa. Some people call me _Miftah_ and some other call me _Hadi_. I obtained an MS degree in physics from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Saudi Arabia. My interest in general is in theoretical condensed matter physics. In particular, I have experience in the following:
- Topological insulators
- Moiré materials
- Density functional theory (DFT)
- Floquet theory
- Machine learning

I started this website to put notes on stuff I learn so I can easily find them whenever I need. I also hope that these notes will be useful for everyone doing similar research in the field.
